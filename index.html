Sloto Club - How do points work

We'd like to tell you more about the Sloto Club feature and the way it works: As soon as you get to 10,000, you get into the club. Points can be earned from purchases, spinning, reaching levels ending with 5 or 0, reaching a new Jackpot stage, collecting your lotto bonus, collecting your mega win, collecting your store bonus and collecting your special bonus. When you are in the club, if you manage to get the second pass in the 7 days allotted time, you'll get 7 more days added. Any points earned above those two passes, will be transformed into coins at the end of the first pass. You can check all the rules in the Sloto Club "Learn More", "Rules" section.

How many gifts can I hold in my gift box?

If you receive a message saying A gift from this friend was "already collected" today, please take a moment to read about how the gifting system currently works: - You can send one gift per day to each friend (24 hour period) - You can collect one gift per day from each friend (24 hour period) - There are no limits to how many wall post shares you can collect (for manual collection)

Game Features

Boosters

By clicking on the Buy Coins button the Payment Page will appear you will also be able to see a second tab next to coins, the Booster section. If you click on the Booster tab you have the chance to buy the Star Dice booster, the White wedge bonanza booster or the Level boom booster.

Lucky Spin

This NEW feature will allow you to multiply any Coin Package by up to x10 times after purchasing it. When selecting a coin package to purchase, after the transaction is complete you will receive the option to play the Lucky Spin, which gives you the chance to multiply your coin amount by up to x10 times! The amount your coin package will be multiplied depends on the combination of symbols you will get when spinning the reels. In case you do not want to purchase the Lucky Spin, you can simply close the pop-up window and receive your regular coin package you just purchased. In case you experience any issue with the Lucky Spin feature, please get in contact our 24-hour Customer Support team.
